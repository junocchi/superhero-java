Superhero Sighting Application

The Superhero Sightings web application is an exciting and innovative platform that allows users to report and track sightings of their favorite superheroes in various locations. Designed for superhero enthusiasts, comic book fans, and curious citizens alike, this application provides a thrilling experience for those who wish to engage with the world of superheroes in a unique way.
